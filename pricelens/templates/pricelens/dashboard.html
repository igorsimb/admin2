PriceLens

PriceLens is a tool for analyzing and visualizing the price history of a stock.

It provides a user-friendly interface for exploring the price history of a stock, allowing users to filter and analyze the data based on various criteria.

With PriceLens, users can easily compare the price history of a stock over time, identify trends and patterns, and make informed investment decisions.
